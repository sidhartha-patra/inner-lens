🔮 Inner Lens

The viral “9 AI prompts”, turned into an app

Decode yourself from a single date.

Inspired by the trend of using structured AI prompts for self-reflection, Inner Lens reads your personality, patterns, relationships, career direction and life purpose from your birth date — blending psychology, numerology logic and pattern mapping. A mirror, not a fortune teller.

🔒 Everything runs in your browser. Your date is never uploaded.

Your nine decoders

Each card is a ready reading. Want the raw prompt to paste into Grok, ChatGPT or Gemini yourself? Hit Show prompt or Copy prompt. Connected your own AI key? Use Generate with AI for a live reading.

How it works

1 · Built-in engine

From your date we compute a real numerology + zodiac profile — Life Path, Personal Year, pinnacles, challenges, sun sign, element and more — then compose each of the nine readings. No account, no network needed.

2 · The original prompts

Every card carries the exact viral prompt, pre-filled with your date, ready to copy into Grok, ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini if you’d rather read it in your own AI tool.

3 · Optional Live AI

Bring your own OpenAI-compatible API key (OpenAI, OpenRouter, Groq…). It’s stored only in your browser and used to generate live readings on demand. Off by default.
